Our services

General Dentistry

General dentistry focuses on maintaining overall oral health. It includes routine check-ups, cleanings, X-rays, and treatments for issues like cavities and gum disease. General dentists help prevent dental problems and keep your smile healthy.

Periodontics

Periodontics focuses on the health of your gums and supporting structures of the teeth. It includes the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of gum diseases such as gingivitis and periodontitis. Periodontists also perform procedures like scaling, root planing, and dental implants to maintain and restore gum health.

Restorative dentistry

Restorative dentistry focuses on repairing and restoring damaged or missing teeth. It includes treatments like fillings, crowns, bridges, and implants to restore the function and appearance of your smile. Restorative dentists help ensure your teeth are healthy, functional, and aesthetically pleasing.
