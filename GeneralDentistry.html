General Dentistry

General dentistry is the foundation of dental care, encompassing a wide range of services designed to maintain and improve overall oral health. This includes routine check-ups, cleanings, X-rays, and various treatments aimed at preventing and addressing common dental issues such as cavities, gum disease, and tooth decay.

During a routine check-up, a general dentist will thoroughly examine your teeth and gums for any signs of problems. This often involves taking X-rays to get a detailed view of your oral health beneath the surface. Regular cleanings help remove plaque and tartar buildup that cannot be eliminated by brushing and flossing alone. This is crucial for preventing cavities and gum disease, which can lead to more serious health issues if left untreated.

General dentistry also includes patient education, emphasizing the importance of good oral hygiene practices at home. Dentists provide guidance on proper brushing and flossing techniques, dietary recommendations, and tips for avoiding habits that can harm your teeth and gums.

Overall, general dentistry plays a vital role in preserving oral health, preventing dental problems, and promoting a lifetime of healthy smiles. Whether you need a routine check-up, a cleaning, or treatment for a specific issue, general dentistry provides the essential care you need to keep your teeth and gums in excellent condition.
